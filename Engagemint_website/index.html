About Us

What is Engagemint?

Engagemint is a peer-to-peer legitimacy protocol, where residents reward others with Civic Digital Native Assets
This gives users ownership over their reputation and sovereignty over their network

A Civic Participation Holostrusture, Engagemint encourages flourishing participation in city proceedings. Establishing legitimacy-networks of beneficial, influential, and trustworthy local community members, Engagemint increases trust and facilitates sense-making. Through providing augmented and improved avenues for civic activism, residents access a larger role in improving their city infrastructure: from Transit Accessibility to Housing Density

The downstream carbon reduction effects from these types of measures are necessary to address our climate catastrophe

Our Mission

Engagemint is a small step towards making government web3, starting at the local level

We're showcasing the potential for reputation systems, based on earned legitimacy, through @GovrnHQ, @mintkudosXYZ, and a @LensProtocol-like composable social graph

Why Engage?

Building these proof-of-legitimacy networks facilitates accountability, from personal relationships to electoral decisions. This is a minuscule step towards onchain identities, but an important bridge between chainspace and meatdimension.

Focus for this project isn’t to more crypto-native tooling –– enough of that exists already. Engagemint is a gentle introduction for everyday users to showcase the multitude of benefits that our new tech bring us
